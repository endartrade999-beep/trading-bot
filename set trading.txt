1. Trend atau Range?

Lihat Daily chart:
📈 Naik terus = UPTREND → Cari BELI aja
📉 Turun terus = DOWNTREND → Cari JUAL aja
↔️ Goyang kiri-kanan = RANGE → Trade pinggir aja

2. Dimana Support & Resistance?

Tarik garis horizontal:
🟢 Support = tempat price bounce naik (minimal 2x)
🔴 Resistance = tempat price mentok turun (minimal 2x)

Tulis di notes:
- Support: [harga]
- Resistance: [harga]

3. Hari ini cari apa?

Kalau UPTREND → Tunggu turun ke support, BUY
Kalau DOWNTREND → Tunggu naik ke resistance, SELL
Kalau RANGE → BUY di bawah, SELL di atas

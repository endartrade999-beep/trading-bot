1️⃣ OVERTRADING (Kesalahan Terbesar!)
Yang Dia Lakukan:

Trading setiap setup yang "kelihatan" bagus
50-100+ trades per day
Tidak sabar tunggu setup perfect
FOMO setiap move

Kerugian:

Commission fees menggerogoti profit
Emotional exhaustion
Banyak loss di setup mediocre
Win rate turun drastis

Pelajaran:
✅ "Less is more" - Quality over quantity
✅ Trade hanya high-probability setup (top 20%)
✅ Target 2-5 trades per day MAX
✅ Skip setup yang tidak jelas
Al Brooks Quote:
"I made more money when I took fewer trades. The best trades are obvious."

2️⃣ TRADING AGAINST THE TREND
Yang Dia Lakukan:

Selalu cari reversal (top/bottom picking)
Counter-trend trading karena "harga terlalu tinggi/rendah"
Fighting the trend = melawan market

Kerugian:

Multiple stop loss hit
Trend bisa berlanjut lebih jauh dari ekspektasi
Stress tinggi

Pelajaran:
✅ "Always In Direction" - Trade with trend
✅ Dalam uptrend = look for LONGS only
✅ Dalam downtrend = look for SHORTS only
✅ Reversal trading = lower probability (skip!)
Al Brooks Quote:
"The market is always 'Always In' long or short. Trade with it, not against it."

3️⃣ MOVING STOP LOSS (Fatal!)
Yang Dia Lakukan:

Set SL, tapi pas mau kena → geser lebih jauh
Harapan "price will come back"
Tidak mau admit wrong

Kerugian:

Small loss jadi BIG LOSS
One bad trade wipe out weeks of profit
Emotional trauma

Pelajaran:
✅ SL is SACRED - Never move wider!
✅ Set SL before entry
✅ Kalau hit = accept loss, move on
✅ Protect capital > ego
Al Brooks Quote:
"Your stop loss is your best friend. Respect it, or the market will punish you."

4️⃣ HOLDING WINNERS TOO LONG
Yang Dia Lakukan:

Target 10-20% profit per trade
Hold for "big move"
Tidak take profit di level logical

Kerugian:

Profit jadi loss (reversal)
Missed multiple good exit
Greed > logic

Pelajaran:
✅ Take profit at resistance/support
✅ Scale out (50% at TP1, 50% at TP2)
✅ Don't be greedy - market gives, market takes
✅ Target realistis (1-3% per trade cukup!)
Al Brooks Quote:
"Pigs get slaughtered. Take your profit when the market gives it to you."

5️⃣ TRADING EVERY TIMEFRAME
Yang Dia Lakukan:

Jump antara 1m, 5m, 15m, 1H, 4H, Daily
Confused by different signals
No consistency

Kerugian:

Analysis paralysis
Conflicting signals
Loss of focus

Pelajaran:
✅ Pick ONE main timeframe (4H or Daily)
✅ Use lower TF hanya untuk entry timing
✅ Consistency = key to learning
✅ Master one TF before adding more
Al Brooks Quote:
"You don't need multiple timeframes to make money. Pick one and master it."

6️⃣ TRADING WITHOUT CONTEXT
Yang Dia Lakukan:

Trade pattern tanpa lihat big picture
"Double bottom" di chart tapi tidak cek trend
Technical analysis tanpa context

Kerugian:

Good pattern di wrong context = loss
High probability setup jadi low probability

Pelajaran:
✅ Context is KING
✅ Check trend di higher TF dulu
✅ Range market ≠ Trending market (different rules!)
✅ Ask: "Where are we in the bigger picture?"
Al Brooks Quote:
"A good pattern in the wrong context is a bad trade."

7️⃣ COMPLICATED SYSTEM (Indicator Hell)
Yang Dia Lakukan:

Pakai 10+ indicators (MACD, RSI, Stoch, Bollinger, Ichimoku, dll)
Waiting for "perfect alignment"
Conflicting signals

Kerugian:

Missed good trades (waiting for all to align)
Confusion, not clarity
Indicators lag (late entry)

Pelajaran:
✅ Price action is enough!
✅ Support/Resistance + Candles + Volume = 90% of what you need
✅ 1-2 indicators MAX (optional!)
✅ Simple > Complex
Al Brooks Quote:
"All you need is price, support/resistance, and your brain. Everything else is noise."
